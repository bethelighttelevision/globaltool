Let's talk about the elephant in the room: **FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out).**

We've all been there. You see a coin pumping, everyone on Twitter is talking about "the moon," and you're tempted to jump in. But here is the problem: most people buy without a plan. They don't know where they will take profit or how much they actually stand to gain.

Successful investors don't guess. They calculate.

## 1. Setting a Realistic "Exit Strategy"
The biggest mistake beginners make is holding for too long. They see 20% profit, but they want 200%. Then the market crashes, and they end up in a loss. 
**Pro Tip:** Always decide your "Take Profit" (TP) level *before* you enter the trade. 

## 2. Understanding the "Fee" Factor
If you buy $1000 of Ethereum and sell it at $1100, you didn't make $100 profit. Once you account for exchange fees and gas, your actual profit might be closer to $80. Knowing your exact net ROI is crucial for long-term survival.

## 3. The Power of "What-If" Scenarios
What if Bitcoin hits $100k? What if it drops to $40k? By running different scenarios through a calculator, you remove the emotion from your trading. You start seeing numbers, not dreams.
