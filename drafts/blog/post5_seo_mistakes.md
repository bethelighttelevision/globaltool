# Post 5: 5 Sneaky SEO Mistakes That Are Killing Your Search Ranking

**Meta Description:** Is your traffic dropping? You might be making these common SEO mistakes without even knowing it. Learn how to fix them and regain your Google rankings.
**Keywords:** Common SEO Mistakes, Fix Search Rankings, Google Penalty 2026, Technical SEO Tips.

---

You check your Google Search Console, and your heart sinks. The red line is going down. You haven’t changed anything, so why is your traffic disappearing?

SEO is a moving target. What worked in 2024 might actually hurt you in 2026. After auditing hundreds of sites, I’ve noticed a pattern. Most people are making the same "sneaky" mistakes that Google's algorithm hates.

Let’s see if you’re guilty of any of these.

## 1. Keyword Stuffing in Alt Tags
We all want to rank for "best coffee beans," but putting that exact phrase into every single image alt tag on your page looks like spam. Google is smart enough to see through this.
**The Fix:** Use descriptive, natural language. "Freshly roasted Arabica beans in a wooden bowl" is much better than "best coffee beans buy coffee beans."

## 2. Neglecting the "Mobile-First" Experience
In 2026, Google indexes the **mobile version** of your site first. If your buttons are too small or your text is hard to read on a phone, your desktop ranking will suffer too. 
*Personal Tip:* Always check your site on a real smartphone, not just a browser emulator.

## 3. Thin Content (Low Value Pages)
Creating hundreds of 200-word pages just to target keywords is a strategy from 2010. Today, Google values "Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness" (E-A-T).
**The Fix:** Quality over quantity. One 2,000-word deep dive is better than ten 200-word fluff pieces.

## 4. Broken Internal Links
A "404 Error" is like a dead end in a maze. If Google’s crawlers hit a dead end, they stop indexing that part of your site.
**ToolSnappy Tip:** Use our SEO Analyzer to quickly spot if your page has technical issues that might be leading to crawl errors.

### Conclusion
Don't panic if your rankings drop. SEO is a marathon. Fix these 5 mistakes, wait a couple of weeks for Google to re-crawl your site, and you’ll likely see that green line moving up again.
