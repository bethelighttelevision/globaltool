## 1. The Power of the H1-H3 Hierarchy
Think of your website like a book. The H1 is your title, and H2s are your chapters. If you have three H1 tags on one page, Google gets confused. It doesn't know what the main topic is. 
**Personal Tip:** Always ensure you have exactly ONE H1 tag that contains your primary keyword.

## 2. Image Alt Tags: The Silent Rankers
Google can’t "see" images like we do. It reads the code. If your images don't have "Alt Tags," you're missing out on a massive amount of Google Images traffic. 
*Fix:* Describe the image accurately in the alt text. Don't just stuff keywords!

## 3. SSL Security is No Longer Optional
If your site starts with `http` instead of `https`, Google will flag it as "Not Secure." In 2026, this is a major ranking killer. Most visitors will bounce immediately if they see that warning.
