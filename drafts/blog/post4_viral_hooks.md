Let's do a quick experiment. Open TikTok or Instagram right now and start scrolling. 

How many videos did you skip within the first second? Probably most of them. Now, why did you stop on that one specific video? 

It wasn't the music. It wasn't the lighting. It was the **Hook**.

In 2026, our attention spans are shorter than ever. If you don't grab someone's brain in the first 2 seconds, you've lost them forever. Here is how the pros do it.

## 1. The "Negative" Hook
Humans are wired to avoid pain more than we seek pleasure. 
*Example:* Instead of saying "How to make money," say "Stop losing money on these 3 things." 
Negative hooks create immediate urgency.

## 2. The "Cliffhanger" Hook
Start in the middle of a story. 
*Example:* "I almost lost my entire business because of this one email..."
Now the viewer *has* to stay to find out what happened.

## 3. The "Unpopular Opinion" Hook
Polarize your audience.
*Example:* "Why I think 9-to-5 jobs are better than freelancing."
People will flock to the comments to agree or argue, which pushes your video to the moon.
